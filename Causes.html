Deforestation It has a negative impact on the environment. The most dramatic impact loss of habitat for millions of species. Rooftop Gardening. Recycling Activity. Smart Products. Plantation & Gardening within a premises.Replanting
Fossil Fuel GHG has far-reaching effects including rising sea levels, increasing wildfires, more extreme weather etc. Plant a tree. Replace your light bulbs. Drive less and drive smart. Buy energy-efficient products. Use less hot water. Ude less air conditioning.
Fertilise Use Nitrous oxide creates nitric oxide when it reacts with oxygen atoms and so reacts with the ozone layer. NO2 is 300 times more dangerous than CO2. Use fruit skins as fertiliser. Ude renewable organic waste as fertilisers.Eco ganeshas, eco pencils, etc.
